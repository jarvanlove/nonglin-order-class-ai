# Security

## Training Safety Rules

- Practice sessions must not require foreign AI tools, VPN, or blocked services.
- Theory can discuss global tools, but should clearly separate awareness from required practice.
- Do not include real student personal data in examples, screenshots, or generated artifacts.
- Avoid publishing school-specific private information unless explicitly approved.

## Content Risk Areas

- Employment guidance and resume examples.
- Tool installation/account setup.
- Student project templates.
- Screenshots of real tools or accounts.
- Generated slides and exported artifacts.

## Review Requirements

Changes touching student setup, accounts, employment advice, or school-specific delivery logistics require manual review before distribution.
